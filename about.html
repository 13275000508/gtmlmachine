    GTML Machine Introduction

    Guantian Mingli Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2002, is located at the wharf of Weiyong Village, Shibi Street, Panyu District, Guangzhou City, Guangdong Province. The factory currently covers an area of 2,680 square meters.

    It is a company specializing in the design, production, sales, and service of vacuum molding machines, with a team of professional senior design engineers and high-quality technical personnel. The main products include new microcomputer-controlled, fully automatic thick film vacuum plastic forming machines, fully automatic high-speed plastic forming machines, single-station thick film plastic forming machines, automobile foot pad and boot pad vacuum plastic forming machines, automatic refrigerator and freezer door lining vacuum plastic forming machines, hot press forming machines, semi-automatic thick film plastic forming machines, large thick film vacuum plastic forming machines, and more. The equipment is known for its stable quality, simple operation, and energy-saving features. It is suitable for vacuum molding of PVC, PS, PP, PET, HIPS, ABS, ABS/PC, ABS/PMMA, and other plastics.

    Since the establishment of the company, with exquisite design, high-quality production technology, and perfect after-sales service, our products have been exported to countries both domestically and abroad.

    Products

    Vacuum Thermoforming Machine

    The vacuum thermoforming machine is primarily used for producing thermoformed products from thick sheets of ABS, HIPS, HDPE, PMMA, PVC, and other materials. The machine is programmed with a PLC system, and the entire operation is controlled and adjusted via a touch screen. The system allows for remote monitoring and parameter modifications. It is simple to operate and ensures stable quality. Its operating process includes: feeding - heating - molding - cutting - entering the product box. The equipment is fast, energy-saving, and labor-saving. It offers stable quality and is easy to operate. We welcome your inquiries and purchase.

    We have independent research and development capabilities and can produce custom vacuum forming equipment according to your needs. Currently, this equipment has been sold to countries such as Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, Cambodia, and others.

    Refrigerator and Freezer Door Liner Machine

    The rapid vacuum forming machine for refrigerator and freezer door liners comes equipped with functions such as bubble blowing, core pulling, mold cooling, demolding, and more. It is suitable for producing refrigerator door liners, freezer door liners, car refrigerator inner tanks, small wine cabinet inner tanks, trays, and more.

    The machine mainly produces HIPS material refrigerator door lining. Operation process: feeding, heating, bubble blowing, core pulling, plastic suction, molding after cooling mode of continuous production. The production speed is fast, save electricity, save labor, is the latest type of equipment. With fast production speed and stable quality. Is your best choice!

    Single-Station Thick Sheet Machine

    The single-station thick sheet vacuum forming machine is mainly used in industries such as automotive parts trays, refrigerator liners, game console casings, medical device exterior components, advertising boards, and more. The machine is stable in performance and easy to operate, featuring a PLC control system that allows for remote monitoring and equipment maintenance.
